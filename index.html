Trusting Us With Your Reputation

Signature Long Haul is the Phoenix that has risen from the ashes of the former Scheduled Long Haul team at Thomas Cook. Scheduled Long Haul employed a team of 70 people and was one the divisions of the Thomas Cook group that remained extremely successful until the collapse of the business.
We are pleased to have taken on a large proportion of the old team and will be offering the same amazing product and service that agents who dealt with Thomas Cook Long Haul have become accustomed to. The Scheduled Long Haul Division was previously known as Thomas Cook Signature and our new brand pays homage to the old and new name.

  • Over 300 years of combined experience

    Our incredible team have years of experience of gained at the Thomas Cook Scheduled Long Haul Team. Some of the team have been booking long haul holidays for over 40 years and enjoy nothing more than designing a complex itinerary.

  • We never say No

    We invite agents to come to us with their most complex enquiries. Our team is not managed based on the number of bookings they do- they are managed based on the quality of the service they give.

  • Hand Picked Resorts

    Everything we feature has been handpicked. Our team will have an in-depth knowledge of every property featured and will be able to advise suitability of each based on your client needs.

  • Beat the Direct Sell

    By focusing on specific product, we can contract directly with the product supplier. Most of our competitors now buy accommodation through a bed bank who keep their own margin adding a layer of additional cost on the price your client pays.

  • Trade Only

    We don’t deal with the public. We know that as agents you have to provide the customer with the principal ATOL holders invoice. It is a fact that even the most honest tour operators cannot stop a client coming to book with them directly in the future once the customer knows who the principal is.

  • Rewards

    The Miles High Club is our reward programme which gives you up to 1.5 miles for every £1 of revenue you do with us. £240,000 revenue a year with us would be enough for you to enjoy a free holiday worth up to £3,300 without paying a penny.

Helping you Sell

We won’t just book the holiday for you- we will give you the tools to be able to promote holidays to your clients, generate leads, incentivise your staff and secure the booking.

Why I Love Travel

Having worked in 4 industries over 16 years there is none that has been as fun and rewarding as travel. I, like many people would say that no 2 working days were the same- until I started in travel!!! Working in travel is genuinely good for the soul. It is challenging, varied, exciting, and sometimes even dramatic. But most of all, it is an industry that makes me feel good about myself!

What to expect from me

My team are amazing, and most of the time you wont even need to speak to me. When I become involved, it will generally be because the issue that needs to be dealt with is very complex. When you contact me, you can be confident that I will be doing everything on my power to address the issues and make your clients holiday as seamless as possible.

Detailed Profile

My career is very important to me- but I realize that I have to take time to relax as well. To help me transition from a busy day at the office to down time- I spend a couple of hours each evening reviewing calls and brainstorming ideas of how to continue to develop processes and my team. I also enjoy gourmet cooking- which helps me learn to focus on detailed instructions and procedures; this is very important in our line of work. Good food also helps me relax and stay healthy.

On Tuesday evenings- I attend a book club for adults who are passionate about historical fiction. Most weekends are spent in my garden or hiking in the mountains with my rescue pup. Eating fresh and healthy food- clearing my mind and keeping my body fit are important to me so that I can focus on my energy effectively at work.

To help me transition from a busy day at the office to down time- I spend a couple of hours each evening reviewing calls and brainstorming ideas of how to continue to develop processes and my team. I also enjoy gourmet cooking- which helps me learn to focus on detailed instructions and procedures; this is very important in our line of work. Good food also helps me relax and stay healthy.

On Tuesday evenings- I attend a book club for adults who are passionate about historical fiction. Most weekends are spent in my garden or hiking in the mountains with my rescue pup. Eating fresh and healthy food- clearing my mind and keeping my body fit are important to me so that I can focus on my energy effectively at work.
